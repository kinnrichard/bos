üîß Fix Summary

Problem: The old logic incorrectly treated any activity with both client_id and job_id (but not loggable_type of 'Job' or 'Client') as cross-references.

Solution: Cross-references should only be activities explicitly marked as such or operations that genuinely span multiple unrelated contexts.

Result: Task renames, updates, and other activities within a specific job are now correctly grouped as "Job Activity" instead of "Cross-reference".
